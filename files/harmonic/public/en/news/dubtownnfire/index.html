Dubtown&Fire: Dub Conference

I&I Dubwise Sound meets Harmonic Soundsystem

On the 2nd of March we played a Soundsystem Conference hosted by I&I Dubwise Soundsystem in Munich.

For the first time since we build our new Subs we could set them up outside of our resident location. With big tunes and such nice boxes we could provide heavy tunes for this session.

Give Thanks fi di invitation and big up to each and everyones help.
